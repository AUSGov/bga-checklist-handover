What does the pattern need to do?

  • Display a series of steps that the user needs to carry out to achieve an outcome.
  • Allow users to easily understand how many steps are involved.
  • Allow users to easily find the information required to complete each step.
  • Allow users to track their progress through the checklist.
  • Allow users to save their progress and return to the checklist at a later date.
  • Display well on both large and small screens.
